About

I have been interested in technology since my early youth when my dad purchased a subscription to Popular Science Magazine for my brother and me. I remember imagining how cool the world would be when scientists could make the machines and computers they were designing. Fast forward a decade later and I was building my first computer with an Intel Pentium III 500MHz processor. From this experience, I found myself helping fix users computers at Best Buy and then as part of the Information Technology Support staff when attending college at California State University, Chico.

A nerd was born.

In 1996, I got the itch for coding by making fan websites for bands and started an online magazine. I remember, how exciting, the first time I had written some code which triggered a song to play within a webpage. After graduation from college, I worked as a Software Engineer within Chevron, AT&T and Comcast. I now had got the taste for developing to 30+ million users and deploying applications across the United States of America and I am hungry for more.

I have transitioned into the role of Engineering Manager and I love it. I appreciate being hands-on both technically with designing and coding and in mentoring team members to work to their fullest potential.

When I am not coding or doing research, you can find me hanging out with my wife or out mountain biking, shooting photography, reading or on the hunt for that perfect cup of coffee.

If you have an exciting start-up, project or role that you feel I would be interested in, please contact me immediately through my LinkedIn profile. Otherwise, follow me through my various social networks such as Twitter or my blogs.
